Sample local requests for the app:

1. GET home page:
   curl http://127.0.0.1:8001/

2. POST chat message:
   curl -X POST http://127.0.0.1:8001/send -d "message=Hello, I want to solve the lunch conversion issue."

3. POST restart interview:
   curl -X POST http://127.0.0.1:8001/restart -d "case=Delivery Complaints"

Notes:
- Use the same cookie/session when running POST requests to preserve the conversation state.
- The app was tested successfully on port 8001.
